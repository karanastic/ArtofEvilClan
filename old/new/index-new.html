About us

First off, let's set the record straight. We are a selective group of Clash of Clans enthusiasts, not a gang. We go to war constantly and cause mayhem on our opponents. This 18+ Clan takes the game seriously with a sense of humour.

The clan originated in Oceanside, California in late 2013. It started out with one man with a dream and a dangerous dose of ambition, RickRox. He soon recruited Smee, Doc G, Beez, and the infamous Karanastic. One by one, more prospects and then members joined from around the globe. Each played a part in a larger role, each serving a purpose. As a result, the clan morphed into a dark brotherhood who mastered the Art of Evil.
