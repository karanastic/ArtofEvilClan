The Rules

Follow these rules to succeed in the Art of Evil clan

1. Adult Only, 18+

2. Use all attacks during war | Even if we are winning, all attacks should be used.

3. Attack your parallel first. (If you are ranked #8 in war, attack their #8 first)

4. If you have not attacked your parallel after 12 hours, another member can use their clean up attack on your parallel.

5. Communicate on chat and donate troops. If you communicate properly, rules 2-4 can be pardoned.

New members are on a 30 day trial period. After that, they will be promoted to Elder. Failure to follow the rules will result in a demotion if Elder or higher, and a swing of the BAN-HAMMER if you are a member.
